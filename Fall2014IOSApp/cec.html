The 2014 BICSI Winter Conference & Exhibition fulfills the conference attendance requirement for RCDD and RITP renewal. All credential holders must check in or register on site by 3 p.m. on Tuesday, February 4, to fulfill the conference attendance requirement.

All conference attendees will receive the following continuing education credits (CECs) for each of the credentials they hold by checking in or registering on site no later than Tuesday, February 4, at 3 p.m.:

RCDD 15
RITP 15
RTPM 12
DCDC 8
ESS Designer 12
OSP Designer 8
Wireless Designer 6
NTS Designer 6
ITS Installer 5
Technician 6
Certified Trainer 12

The CEC totals listed above do not include the pre-conference seminars.

To view the CECs for Pre-conference Seminars and concurrent sessions, visit the conference schedule.

CECs earned from the Fall Conference and/or Pre-conference Seminar attendance will appear on your transcript approximately 30 days after the conference.
